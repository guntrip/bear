Bear Ferry Simulator
Winter is nearing and the bears are getting fat. This year, the bears have been so successful that they are too fat to cross the river. You and your flimsy boat are the only hope for the bears to start their hibernation.
Click on a bear to pick it up, click again to place (or drop) it. Select and arrange the bears to keep your boat stable and above water.
When you're happy, click on the boat to push it across the river. When the bears have disembarked, you can click on the boat again to push it back.
